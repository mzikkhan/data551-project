# Team Work Contract

This document outlines the logistics, expectations, and standards for our team collaboration on the Sustainability Dashboard project.

## Team Logistics

### Distribution of Work
Work will be converted into a list of tasks and distributed evenly among group members based on each task's estimated duration and the skill sets of the members.

### Work Hours
Each member is expected to contribute approximately **3.5 hours per week** to the project.

### Meetings
- **Frequency:** Twice a week.
- **Location:** Remote.
- **Agendas:** Decided collaboratively by all members.
- **Minutes:** Automatically recorded by Slack AI.

### Availability
- **Unavailable Times:** Fridays (family obligations/other commitments).
- **Style of Working:** Remote.
- **Contribution Summaries:** Instead of daily stand-ups, we will maintain a shared contributions file that summarizes work done each week.

## Team Standards and Expectations

### Quality of Work
Each team member expects 10/10 quality from themselves and each other.

### Work Habits
- **Timeliness:** Finish work on time and attend meetings punctually.
- **Communication:**
    - Communicate openly about thoughts, suggestions, and blockers.
    - Provide early warning of any blockers.
    - Clear communication of work done.
    - Maintain clear indications of the use of AI.
    - Communicate issues in the group in a clear and respectful manner.
- **Coordination:** Coordinate to avoid repetitive work or clashes.

### Procedures
- **Meeting Attendance:** Attend all scheduled meetings unless prior notice is given.
- **Decision Making:** Decisions will be made collaboratively by the team.
- **Quality Control & Internal Deadlines:** Adhere to internal deadlines to ensure high-quality output.
- **Conflict Resolution:** Conflicts will be managed and resolved through open communication and mutual agreement.
- **Version Control:** All code and project management will be handled via Git/Version Control.
- **Leadership:** Agreement on who will submit assignments or lead meetings will be decided on a rotating or as-needed basis.

## Agreement
By participating in this project, all team members agree to abide by the terms of this contract.
